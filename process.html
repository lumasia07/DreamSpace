Our Design Process

A transparent, collaborative journey from concept to completion, tailored to bring your vision to life.

01

Initial Consultation

We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your needs, preferences, and aspirations for the space.

What to Expect

  • 1-2 hour meeting at your space or virtually
  • Discussion of functional requirements
  • Review of inspiration images
  • Budget and timeline overview

Deliverables

  • Project scope document
  • Preliminary design direction
  • Proposal for next steps
02

Concept Development

Our team translates your vision into tangible design concepts through sketches, mood boards, and material selections.

Our Approach

  • Space planning and layout options
  • Color palette development
  • Material and finish selections
  • Furniture and lighting concepts

Collaboration

  • Presentation of 2-3 design directions
  • Interactive feedback sessions
  • Refinement of selected concept
03

Design Refinement

We develop the approved concept into detailed design documentation for implementation.

Technical Development

  • Detailed floor plans and elevations
  • Custom millwork and furniture drawings
  • Lighting and electrical plans
  • Material specifications

Procurement

  • Furniture and fixture selection
  • Fabric and finish sampling
  • Budget review and adjustments
04

Implementation

Our team oversees the physical realization of your design, ensuring quality and attention to detail.

Construction Phase

  • Contractor coordination
  • Regular site visits
  • Quality control checks
  • Problem-solving as needed

Installation

  • Furniture and accessory placement
  • Final styling and finishing touches
  • Client walkthrough
05

Reveal & Follow-up

We celebrate the completed project with you and ensure your complete satisfaction.

Final Reveal

  • Professional photography
  • Final walkthrough
  • Care instructions
  • Warranty information

Ongoing Support

  • 30-day follow-up
  • Maintenance recommendations
  • Future project planning
